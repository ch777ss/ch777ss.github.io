Opportunity is not for the prepared

2026-01-27

Opportunity is not for the prepared.
It is for the bold.

We were taught to wait until we are ready.
To understand first, then act.

Reality works differently.

Many people who get opportunities don’t know more —
they just step in earlier.

Ignorance doesn’t stop action.
Hesitation does.

Most opportunities are not given.
They are taken.
